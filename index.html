The Values Sort

The exercise comes from Shalom Schwartz's Theory of Basic Human Values. I've now done it with more than 200 people. The conversation it starts is always worth having.

True friendship with my dogs and with the sheep of the field, with the natural world. Perhaps even true friendship with the God of my childhood?"

About Nick

I'm from a little, rural bit of Oregon and now I live in a different little, rural bit of Oregon.

We hung our coffee-shingle in 2008. This work has taught me things about community and impermanence that I'm still sorting through.

I'm a member of a lay monastic order with my wife. We live with our kids on a few acres with chickens, yellow labs named Chuck & Barb, and a teardrop camper I record podcasts in.
